CYA Recreational Soccer

Development Clinics

Once-a-week training for beginners through advanced players, U4–U13.

A fun, friendly, engaging environment built to develop each player’s skills and love for the game through skill activities and reinforcement games. This is a once-a-week “Practice & Play” program coached by CYA staff coaches, open to brand-new players and anyone looking to sharpen the skills they already have.

Each session runs 5 practices on Thursday evenings. Staff may ask a player to play up an age group if a session isn’t challenging enough for them.

What Players Will Work On

Individual ball mastery, technical elements like turns and moves, individual and group tactics through small games, offensive and defensive tactics in 1v1, 2v2, and 3v3 situations, plus a variety of fun, creatively designed games.

What To Wear, What To Bring

Athletic wear, layer if you’d like · shin guards under soccer socks · a full water bottle · a soccer ball · no jewelry · sneakers or indoor cleats (no outdoor cleats)
