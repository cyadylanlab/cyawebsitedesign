Fall 2026 Calendar

Key dates, jersey info, and game days for the season.

New jersey cycle as of Fall 2025: jerseys are included in your registration fee at no additional cost. Jersey numbers are assigned, players and parents can’t choose a number.

If your player’s jersey from Spring 2026 still fits, there’s no need to pick up a new one for Fall 2026.

Players with an Active registration status should hear from their coach the week of August 30, if not before. This depends on volunteer coach registration, so timing can vary. Players with a Pending status are not eligible to practice or play.
