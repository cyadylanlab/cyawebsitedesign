CYA Recreational Soccer

Play With Purpose

Chantilly’s home for youth recreational soccer, ages 4 to 19, player-centered programs focused on technical fundamentals, age-appropriate fitness, and fun.

Est. 1971
Why Recreational Soccer

Coached By Your Community

Every CYA team is led by a volunteer, a parent, a neighbor, someone who shows up because they care about the kids on the field, not a paycheck. Backed by CYA and Valor staff, it's what keeps this game affordable and close to home.

CYA Recreational Benefits

Community based programs for family convenience
U4 through U19 teams coached by CYA staff-trained volunteers
Weekly U7–U8 Boys & Girls Academy sessions run by professional CYA and Valor staff coaches, free
Consistent training night and time each week, on turf
Supplemental training for players already registered
Fun, fast-paced, soccer-related games and activities
Builds motor skills, cognitive ability, and peer relationships
Builds basic soccer skills and a lasting love for the game
