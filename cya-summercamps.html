Why Choose Valor Summer Camps

Professional Coaching: training led by experienced Valor coaches, with an emphasis on individual development and game understanding
Skill Development: dribbling, passing, shooting, finishing, and position-specific techniques
Engaging Games & Competitions: small-sided games and challenges that build creativity, confidence, and sportsmanship
Fitness & Conditioning: age-appropriate activities that keep players active and match-ready
Team Building: leadership, communication, and teamwork in every session

Camp Location

All camps are held at Sully Highlands Park, 13808 Wall Road, Herndon, VA 20171.

2026 Summer Camp Schedule

July 13–16: World Cup Camp
Tournament-style play inspired by international competition, with team challenges and game-based learning.

July 20–23: Complete Player Camp
Develop well-rounded technical and tactical skills across every area of the game.

July 27–30: Striking & Finishing Camp
A high-intensity camp built to sharpen the art of scoring, developing confident, composed attackers who can finish under pressure.

July 27–30: Goalkeeping Camp
Specialized training all week for goalkeepers, working closely with one of Valor’s Goalkeeper Directors. Runs alongside Striking & Finishing the same week.
