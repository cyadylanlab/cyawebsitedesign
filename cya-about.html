Our Purpose

To educate youth to play soccer, and live, with purpose.

Our Mission

CYA Soccer gives every player, from a four year old touching a ball for the first time to a teenager chasing a college roster spot, a real path forward. That path runs on volunteer coaches from our own community, backed by certified training, access to our Fieldhouse facilities, and cross-training for strength, conditioning, and injury prevention.

Our competitive travel teams play under Virginia Valor FC, CYA's travel soccer partner. The two clubs are closely coordinated, sharing staff and facilities, and several of our junior trainers are Valor players who volunteer their own time coaching at CYA. If you see Valor branding alongside CYA's around our programs, that's why, it's one community working across both.

We're proud that this is a community club. Parents coach because they want to, not because it's their job, and that shows in how much they care about getting it right. Our role is to back that care with real structure and support, so every player gets a program worth showing up for, whatever their goals look like.

We ARE Chantilly Soccer. We ARE CYA.

Our Philosophy

We believe every kid deserves a place to play. Some show up to have fun and make friends on Saturday mornings. Some are working toward playing in college. Both belong here, and we build our recreational and travel programs to take both paths just as seriously.

Being run by the community doesn't mean going without real structure. Our coaches are parents and neighbors, not hired trainers, but we back them with certified coaching education, real facilities, and a development pathway that carries a player from their first touch at U4 through competitive travel soccer. That combination, neighbors coaching neighbors' kids, supported by real professional resources, is what lets us offer a genuinely good environment for every kind of player and every kind of goal.

We've held ourselves to that standard since 1971, and we're proud of it.
