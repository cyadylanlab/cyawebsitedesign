Refund Policy

Our registration fee refund policy, in full.

Effective August 2024

Introduction

The CYA Registration Fee Refund Policy is designed to offer our members a consistent refund procedure across all of our recreational sports.

Changes In Policy

CYA reserves the unlimited right to revise any part of the Refund Policy set forth in this document. All changes will be identified and introduced to our members on the CYA webpage.

Stated Policy

A full fee refund, less $5 to account for system transaction fees, will be granted prior to the end of the regular registration period. The regular registration period varies by sport, dates for each sport are on the CYA website in the “registration” section. For sports without a late fee, the regular registration period is defined on the relevant registration page.

If a withdrawal request comes after team assignment, the $35 CYA Admin Fee will be withheld in addition to the $5 system fee.

Service fees are non-refundable.

Once the Late Registration Period begins, all refunds must be approved by the Sport Coordinator. Late fees are non-refundable, and the Sport Coordinator can deny any refund once late registration begins.

No refunds are made for cancellations due to inclement weather or other circumstances beyond our control.

Refund Request Procedure

Requests made during the regular registration period must be submitted in writing to the CYA Registrar at sports@chantillyyouth.org. Requests made after the regular registration period must be submitted in writing to the Sport Coordinator.

Final Dates To Request A Refund

Spring Sports Season: June 30
Fall Sports Season: November 30
Winter Sports Season: March 31
